What we do

96kliveteam enters the pro-audio market as an experienced newcomer. We aim to offer boutique services providing tailored packages to our touring hire clients.

We will work to develop an equipment supply service alongside our hire offering as we grow our customer base and opportunities.

Without a legacy hire stock our systems are designed for current technology with a focus on quality and efficiency.

Our initial offering is focussed on hire systems; we have built a rider friendly inventory all packaged with intelligent infrastructure to make our systems pack well and provide flexibility whatever the application. We are now carrying PA, mixing consoles, power distribution, wireless systems and production packages.
